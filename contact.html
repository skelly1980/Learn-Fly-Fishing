Contact Us

Fly Fishing is for you!

I have been fly fishing for a very long time. One thing I know from having worked in the fly fishing industry for most of my life is I enjoy getting new people into the sport. I have always believed that the way to improve the sport is to teach it to as many people as possilbe. The truth is that fly fishing is for everyone and anyone who wants to learn I am happy to teach. Have you tried fly fishing before and gotten frustrated? Reach out to me and I will help you however I can. Sometimes people new to the sport can be intimidated to ask questions because they beleive that experienced anglers don't want to give away knowlegde they spent years learning. For me this is not the case. If I can teach you and you get enjoyment out of the sport all the better. Hopefully, you will then take the knowledge you've learned and pass it along to someone else. It's a wonderful sport and the only way it stays alive is by bringing new people into it.

Got a question? We'd love to hear from you. Send us a message and we'll respond as soon as possible.
